Part 2 of the PostgreSQL 18 series on Amazon Aurora and Amazon RDS covers security, monitoring, and developer features, including deprecation of MD5 password authentication, enhanced parallel worker monitoring via new pg_stat_database and pg_stat_statements columns, and logical replication conflict tracking. It also introduces optimizer statistics preservation during pg_upgrade, the uuidv7() function for timestamp-ordered identifiers, parallel streaming as the default, and idle slot auto-invalidation.
