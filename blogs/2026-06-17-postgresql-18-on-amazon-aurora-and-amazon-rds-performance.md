PostgreSQL 18 on Amazon Aurora and Amazon RDS brings performance improvements including skip scan optimization for multicolumn indexes that delivers up to 21x performance gains, enhanced EXPLAIN output for materialized CTEs, automatic removal of unnecessary self-joins in ORM-generated queries, and vacuum and autovacuum improvements. New autovacuum controls include the autovacuum_vacuum_max_threshold and vacuum_truncate parameters.
